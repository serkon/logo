Pagination

Pagination breaks related content or data into multiple pages and allows a user to navigate through them.

  • Overview
  • Demo
  • Formatting
  • Behavior

Overview

Large numbers of items can overwhelm users and prevent them from finding what they’re looking for. Separating the items out into separate pages can help users focus on a few choices at a time.

When to use

You don’t need to hide items behind pages when they can all fit in a single manageable view. Generally, use pagination if you have more than 10 or so items to display.
