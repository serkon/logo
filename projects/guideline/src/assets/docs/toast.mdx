    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>
      It is a small message that appears on the screen and disappears after a few seconds. It is usually a feedback on
      an interaction where the current activity remains visible and interactable.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="wtu">
    <h3>When to use</h3>
    <p>
      - Use when you want to view a short success message.
    </p>
    <p>
      - Use when you want to display an error or warning message
    </p>

  <h3>
    Alignment
  </h3>
  <img src="../assets/img/docs_toast_4.png" alt=""/>
  <p>Text should be in the center of the container.</p>
  <img src="../assets/img/docs_toast_5.png" alt=""/>
  <p>Text must not be aligned to the left of the container.</p>
  <h3>Modifier</h3>
  <img src="../assets/img/docs_toast_6.png" alt=""/>
  <h3>Placement</h3>
  <p>Toast messages slide in and out from the top right of the screen.</p>
  <h3>Sizing</h3>
  <p>Toast messages can be resized with their content. The information text should be brief and clear, not more than 3
    lines if not necessary.</p>
  <div class="behavior">
    <h2>Behavior</h2>
    <p>
      Toasts turn off automatically after five seconds on the screen. Toasts can also be dismissed manually. If the
      toast content is important, they can be configured to be dismissed only by the user.
    </p>
  </div>
