  <div class="overview">
    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>
      Bullet points help break up large blocks of text, make complex articles and blog posts easier to grasp, and make
      key information stand out.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="wtu">
    <h3>When to use</h3>
    <p>-Use to show text in a specific order.</p>
    <p>-Used to make a complex concept understandable.</p>
    <p>-Use bullet point when the order of the list items does not matter.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="wtnu">
    <h3>When not to use</h3>
    <p>-Don’t overuse bulleted lists, as they can lose their effectiveness.</p>
    <p>-Don't use bullet points for a header.</p>
  </div>

    <p>-Don’t use a bullet point for only one item.</p>
    <p>-Keep each item succinct for scannability.</p>
    <p>-Do not put a dot at the end of a sentence or word.</p>
